FAQs

1. How do I know if I need therapy?

-
If you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, low, or stuck—or simply want to better understand yourself—therapy can help. You don't need a crisis to benefit from mental health support.

2. What is mental wellness?

+
Mental wellness is a state of well-being where you can cope with life's stresses, work productively, and contribute to your community. It involves emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

3. What types of therapy do you offer?

+
We offer various therapeutic approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), mindfulness-based therapy, couples therapy, and family therapy, tailored to your specific needs.

4. What can I expect in a therapy session?

+
In your first session, we'll discuss your goals and concerns in a safe, confidential environment. Ongoing sessions focus on developing coping strategies, exploring thoughts and feelings, and working toward your therapeutic goals.
